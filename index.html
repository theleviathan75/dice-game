🛑 How to play:


Two players go at it in a dice game. First person to get to 50 wins! Hit the 'roll dice' button to start rolling. Points will accumulate in the current section. Keep rolling to accumulate points, but DON'T roll a 1. Your current score will reset if you do. If you've been lucky not rolling a 1, you can hold those points to keep by pressing the 'hold' button. First person to get to 50 wins! Good luck!! To play again, press the new game button!
